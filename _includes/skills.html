Cooking

I'm a generally modest person, but I'll admit cooking is an area in which I excel. I started young and have cooked and baked on a regular basis ever since. I worked for a few summers in a camp kitchen making birthday cakes, cooking hundreds of pancakes for breakfasts, doing fast-paced prep work, and admittedly doing a lot of dishes too. My senior year of high school (a residential school, the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics), I baked bread each week and sold it for spending money. I also spent a summer working in a bakery where my primary culinary task was preparing their signature lunch special: chicken salad. The regular bread baker was also able to take his first vacation in a decade, trusting me to bake hundreds of loaves of bread in his place while he was gone.

More recently, I was ecstatic to help StandUp For Kids Phoenix with a pancake breakfast fundraiser. This reminded me how much I love to cook for a large scale, and how fufilling it is. If you know a charitable organization (or even a bereaved family) that needs food for any reason get in touch with me right now. I can just about guarantee I'll be happy to help.
